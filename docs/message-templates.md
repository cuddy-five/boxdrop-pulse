# Message templates

## Initial rating request

"Thanks! Would you be willing to share a quick review? {review_url}"

## Negative score reply
"Thanks for letting us know. A manager will reach out to make this right."

## Owner alert (SMS)
"New low rating ({rating}/10) from {name} {phone}. Summary: {summary}"
